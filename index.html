Vision 2030 Overview

Zimbabwe's roadmap to becoming an upper middle-income economy by 2030, focused on inclusive growth, innovation, and resilience.

Vision & Mission

To transform Zimbabwe into a prosperous, empowered, upper-middle-income society by 2030.

Inclusive economic growth

Modern infrastructure development

Good governance and rule of law

Environmental sustainability

Development Path

Zimbabwe's journey to Vision 2030 through strategic development programs.

Focused on stabilizing the economy, restoring confidence, and laying the foundation for growth.

Building momentum through structural reforms and infrastructure development.

NDS2 (2026-2030)

Accelerating growth and achieving Vision 2030 goals.

Development Programs

Zimbabwe's strategic programs for economic recovery and sustainable development.

TSP

2018-2020

Transitional Stabilisation Programme

A short-term economic recovery plan to stabilize the economy, restore confidence, and lay the foundation for sustainable growth.

Key Achievements

Reduced inflation and fiscal deficit, introduced currency reforms

Focus Areas

Fiscal consolidation, monetary reforms, structural changes

NDS1

2021-2025

National Development Strategy 1

A medium-term plan focused on accelerating economic growth and creating a foundation for sustainable development.

Key Achievements

GDP growth, infrastructure development, improved social services

Focus Areas

13 thematic clusters including economic growth, agriculture, and infrastructure

NDS2

2026-2030

National Development Strategy 2

The final phase of Zimbabwe's journey to Vision 2030, focused on achieving upper middle-income status.

Expected Outcomes

7% GDP growth, 2 million jobs, halved poverty rate

Focus Areas

15 thematic clusters expanding on NDS1, with emphasis on innovation

Economic Sectors

Key sectors driving Zimbabwe's economic growth and development under Vision 2030.

Agriculture

Zimbabwe's agricultural sector is a cornerstone of the economy, providing livelihoods for the majority of the population.

Food Security Initiatives

Supporting smallholder farmers and promoting climate-smart agriculture

Irrigation Projects

Expanding irrigation infrastructure to reduce dependence on rainfall

Success Stories

Showcasing achievements in agricultural productivity and exports

Mining

Zimbabwe is endowed with rich mineral resources, making mining a significant contributor to the economy and exports.

Value Addition

Promoting downstream processing of minerals for increased value

Environmental Regulations

Ensuring responsible mining practices and rehabilitation

Investment Opportunities

Attracting foreign and domestic investment in the mining sector

Tourism

Tourism is a vital sector for Zimbabwe, showcasing the country's natural beauty, cultural heritage, and hospitality.

Destination Highlights

Promoting Victoria Falls, national parks, and cultural sites

Infrastructure Projects

Developing airports, roads, and accommodation facilities

Events & Expos

Hosting international events to showcase Zimbabwe's tourism offerings
